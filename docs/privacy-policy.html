Privacy Policy

App: Memorise Quran
Last updated: 8 May 2026

Overview

Memorise Quran is a personal tool to help users memorise the Quran verse by verse. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is used and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, and we do not know who you are.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your memorisation progress (which verses you have marked as memorised) is saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. It is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

The app fetches Quran text and audio from the following public APIs:

  • AlQuran Cloud API (alquran.cloud) — provides Arabic text and English translations.
  • Islamic Network CDN (cdn.islamic.network) — provides audio recitations.

These requests contain no personal data — only the surah and verse number requested. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
